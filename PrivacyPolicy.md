# Privacy Policy for JUST University Student Services Enhancer

**Last Updated:** December 19, 2025

## Overview

JUST University Student Services Enhancer ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

The Extension **does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data**. Specifically:

- ❌ We do not collect your name, email, or student ID
- ❌ We do not track your browsing history
- ❌ We do not collect your academic records or grades
- ❌ We do not transmit any data to external servers
- ❌ We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- ❌ We do not share any information with third parties

### Local Storage Only

The Extension uses local browser storage (localStorage) exclusively for:

1. **User Preferences**: Storing your theme preference (light/dark mode)
2. **Recent Pages**: Keeping track of recently accessed menu items for quick access (stored only on your device)

This data:
- ✅ Stays entirely on your local device
- ✅ Is never transmitted anywhere
- ✅ Can be cleared by clearing your browser data
- ✅ Is not accessible to us or any third party

## Permissions Explanation

The Extension requires the following permissions:

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose**: To save your preferences (theme settings) and recent pages locally
- **Scope**: Data never leaves your device

### Host Permission (services.just.edu.jo)
- **Purpose**: To enhance and modify the appearance of JUST student services pages
- **Scope**: Only works on JUST university website pages
- **Function**: Injects CSS styles and JavaScript for improved UI/UX

## Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our Extension. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, communicate with, or send data to any third-party services or APIs.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Uninstall the Extension at any time
- Clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser's local storage
- Request information about what data is stored (which is only local preferences)

## Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through:
- GitHub Repository: https://github.com/Ti-03/JUST-Extension
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/Ti-03/JUST-Extension/issues

## Disclaimer

This Extension is an unofficial tool created by students for students. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected with Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST).

By installing and using the JUST University Student Services Enhancer extension, you consent to this privacy policy.
